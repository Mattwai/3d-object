3d Object Inspiration

For my 3d object originally i was inspired by lightsabers. I then decided on creating a type of sword through the idea of a lightsaber. One of the first things that came to mind when envisioning swords was the sword Excalibur that King Arthur pulled out of the stone in English Mythology. When researching the sword and the medieval time period I noticed the dominance of the colors red and gold which influenced the color theme I chose for my sword.
